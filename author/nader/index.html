Hello and welcome to my blog!

I'm Nader Debbabi, a Tunisian software engineering student, a full stack web developer and a freelancer.

I have been working with Java, C#, Spring framework and .NET framework for quite some time with an important focus on REST APIs, microservices and distributed architectures. And for frontend development, Angular has always been my weapon of choice.

I started this blog, basically to share what I have been learning, to provide some solutions to problems that I have been struggling with, hoping that they may be beneficial to someone, and more importantly to have some public feedback about my approach of implementing certain use cases.

Outside of the software development realm, I'm a big movies geek, an avid electronic music listener and just a guy who enjoys spending time with family and friends. More information? 🤔 Feel free to contact me! 😅
